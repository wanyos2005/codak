About Our Company

The purpose of this group is to bring together owners of carwash businesses under the Umbrella of the Carwash Owners and Detailers Association of Kenya(CODAK). The association will undertake the following activities:
1. Staff management Develop a database for employees working in carwashes plus those intending to work in car washes. This will help us weed out rogue employees who move around carwashes because as owners we do not have visibility. Come up with a short-term course certified by NITA for training car wash attendants. The course Syllabus will include, carwash management and supervision, Motor Vehicle driving, machine handling, health and safety, Entrepreneurship and practical work. Upon completion trainees will have a certified certificate from the association.
2. Machine repair and maintenance Identify and work with knowledgeable and objective car wash machine garages who are not exploitative. In the long run come, we need to come up with our own association garage that will be tasked with repair and general maintenance of machine and pipes. This will minimize cost of repair and general maintenance from our members.
3. Emerging technologies We will recommend new technologies that are ecofriendly and cost effective to our business. This will be done through regular events Currently, we are engaging companies dealing in carwash accessories and machines to organize events and showcase what is new and valuable in the carwash space.
4. Dispute Resolution Act as an arbiter or go between where there is a dispute between carwash owners, attendants, car owners, landlords and Government
5. Car wash Management Train members on managing carwash business in the areas of human capital management, costing, book keeping, and managing losses
6. Act as a link to county and national government
7. Champion the welfare of the stakeholders
